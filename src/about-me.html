Henry Savich

Hello! My name's Henry and I work at the Tennessee State Collaborative on Reforming Education as a data specialist on the topic of education-to-work.


I'm going to meander a bit on this page, for the executive summary please visit my LinkedIn


• Where I Started •

I am a proud Marylander. I was born in Baltimore but spent only a few unmemorable months there before moving to a more suburbant environ in Howard County. I was privileged to grow up in a lively household with three siblings and two dogs.


I spent most of my early childhood playing video games, reading books, avoiding soccer practice, and exploring outdoors. My first fascination was animals, and my first dream job was a zookeeper. While part of it was an interest in nature in general, I think yet more of it was just about how many fun facts there were about animals for my young self to learn. I practically learned to read from reading and rereading the Kingisher First Animal Encylopedia and when I had nearly memorized that I asked my parents for a book with even more animals (and received The Encyclopedia of Animals which I spent many dozens more hours with).
